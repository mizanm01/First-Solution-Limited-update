Company Overview

First Solutions Limited (FSL) is a professionally managed providing integrated Security Services, Facility Management Solutions, Electronic Security Systems, and Office Stationery & Supply Services to banks, financial institutions, multinational companies, corporate offices, factories, and government organizations across Bangladesh.
The company is recognized for its professional standards, operational discipline, reliability, and commitment to client satisfaction. FSL combines experienced leadership, trained manpower, and modern security solutions to deliver dependable and cost-effective services tailored to institutional and corporate requirements.

About Us

The management team of First Solutions Limited consists entirely of experienced former professionals from G4S Bangladesh, bringing international standards of security operations, compliance, and corporate governance into the organization.
The company is led by Mr. Selim Chowdhury, former Managing Director of G4S Bangladesh. Under his leadership, FSL has developed a reputation for professionalism, disciplined operations, efficient service delivery, and strong client relationship management.
The management team focuses on continuous improvement, operational excellence, compliance, and maintaining high standards in every aspect of service delivery.
